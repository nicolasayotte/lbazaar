## Prerequisites

- The platform is deployed and accessible
- A student account `[Student_A]` exists and is authenticated
- A paid class `[Class_General]` exists with a JPY price and a valid ADA equivalent (when the exchange rate service is available)
- `[Student_A]` has not yet purchased `[Class_General]`
- Both payment paths (ADA and credit card) are configured and available unless a specific scenario states otherwise

## F-04.1: Parallel Payment UI Presence

---

### TS-04.01: Both ADA and credit card payment buttons are visible simultaneously

**Traces to:** F-04.1
**Category:** HAPPY PATH
**Criticality:** BLOCKING

**GIVEN:**
- `[Student_A]` is logged in
- `[Class_General]` is a paid class with both payment paths available

**WHEN:**
- `[Student_A]` navigates to the detail page for `[Class_General]`

**THEN:**
- A "Buy with ADA" button is visible
- A "Pay with Credit Card" button is visible
- Both buttons are present on the page simultaneously

---

### TS-04.02: Neither payment option is visually subordinated

**Traces to:** F-04.1
**Category:** HAPPY PATH
**Criticality:** HIGH

**GIVEN:**
- `[Student_A]` is viewing the detail page for `[Class_General]`
- Both payment buttons are visible

**WHEN:**
- `[Student_A]` observes the payment options

**THEN:**
- Both buttons are visible (not hidden or `aria-hidden`)
- Both buttons have equal visual prominence (both use `MuiButton-root` class — no visual demotion of either option)
- The credit card button is not disabled

**NOTES:** The ADA button may be disabled when no wallet is connected (`!walletAPI`) — this is legitimate wallet-level gating, not service unavailability. TS-04.02 must NOT assert the ADA button is enabled; only that it is visible and has equal visual treatment.

---

### TS-04.03: Unauthenticated visitor does not see active payment options

**Traces to:** IMPLICIT — no direct spec scenario
**Category:** EDGE CASE
**Criticality:** MEDIUM

**GIVEN:**
- A visitor is not logged in

**WHEN:**
- The visitor navigates to the detail page for `[Class_General]`

**THEN:**
- No "Buy with ADA" button is visible
- No "Pay with Credit Card" button is visible
- The page loads successfully with the course title and description visible
- A login affordance (e.g., navigation link to `/login`) is present

**NOTES:** The detail page conditionally renders payment buttons only when `auth.user` is present. Unauthenticated visitors see the course information but not the purchase controls.

## F-04.2: Payment Path Degradation

---

### TS-04.04: ADA button disabled with explanation when conversion unavailable

**Traces to:** F-04.2
**Category:** FAILURE
**Criticality:** HIGH

**GIVEN:**
- `[Student_A]` is logged in
- The exchange rate service is unavailable (`price_in_ada === null`)

**WHEN:**
- `[Student_A]` views the detail page for `[Class_General]`

**THEN:**
- The "Buy with ADA" button is disabled
- An explanation such as "ADA payment temporarily unavailable" is visible near the disabled button

---

### TS-04.05: Credit card payment functional when ADA is unavailable

**Traces to:** F-04.2
**Category:** HAPPY PATH
**Criticality:** HIGH

**GIVEN:**
- `[Student_A]` is logged in
- The exchange rate service is unavailable (`price_in_ada === null`)

**WHEN:**
- `[Student_A]` views the payment options for `[Class_General]`

**THEN:**
- The "Pay with Credit Card" button is NOT disabled
- `[Student_A]` can still initiate a credit card payment
- The JPY price is still displayed

**NOTES:** ADA service degradation must not affect the credit card payment path. The two paths degrade independently.

---

### TS-04.06: Credit card button disabled with explanation when Stripe unavailable

**Traces to:** F-04.2
**Category:** FAILURE
**Criticality:** HIGH

**GIVEN:**
- `[Student_A]` is logged in
- Stripe is unavailable (`stripeAvailable === false`)

**WHEN:**
- `[Student_A]` views the detail page for `[Class_General]`

**THEN:**
- The "Pay with Credit Card" button is disabled
- An explanation such as "Credit card payment temporarily unavailable" is visible near the disabled button

---

### TS-04.07: ADA payment functional when Stripe is unavailable

**Traces to:** F-04.2
**Category:** HAPPY PATH
**Criticality:** HIGH

**GIVEN:**
- `[Student_A]` is logged in
- Stripe is unavailable (`stripeAvailable === false`)
- The exchange rate service is available (ADA price is computed)

**WHEN:**
- `[Student_A]` views the payment options for `[Class_General]`

**THEN:**
- The "Buy with ADA" button is visible (may be disabled if no wallet connected, but not due to Stripe status)
- `[Student_A]` can still initiate the ADA payment path

**NOTES:** Stripe service degradation must not affect the ADA payment path. The two paths degrade independently.
